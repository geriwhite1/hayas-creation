Seasonal Trends and Lawn Dresses

Regional climate significantly influences the choice of fabric and design in lawn dresses. In warmer climates, lightweight and breathable fabrics are essential. Countries like Bangladesh and Sri Lanka, where humidity is high, demand lawn dresses with loose cuts and airy designs.

In colder regions, lawn dresses are often paired with warmer accessories or even modified into velvet dress designs for a luxurious touch. Hayas Creation has successfully addressed this need by offering versatile designs that cater to various climates, ensuring year-round comfort and style.
